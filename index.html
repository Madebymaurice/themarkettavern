Agenda

The team at TMT knows how to have a good time, and offers live music and events every month depending on what there is to celebrate! There are weekly events such as a Happy Burger Night (every Thursday), the famous Pub Quiz (every Sunday at 8:30pm) and a Cocktail Night (every Friday) where you have a selection of four Cocktails each for 5€. Sporting events don’t go by unnoticed at The Market Tavern. With three large HD Screens with Sky Sports and BT Sports, no game is ever missed! Having opened the year of the 2015 Rugby World Cup, TMT has built quite the reputation for being THE best place in the Chartrons to watch a game of football, rugby and even cricket! Don’t hesitate to call to make sure your match will be show!

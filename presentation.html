THE BORDEAUX GASTROPUB

Opened in the start of 2015, The Market Tavern Bordeaux GastroPub is on the forefront of modern pub food and tasty craft beers on tap. Inspired by the modern gastro-pubs of London, TMT is a small family oriented establishment in the heart of Bordeaux's chic and historic Chartrons district.

Read about the Pub Group

Welcoming you with open arms seven days a week from 11am until 2am, this family owned pub has 40 seats inside and 40 seats on a terrace with table service all day. During lunchtimes you can enjoy the "Formule du Midi" that includes a selection of different starters, main courses and desserts that changes every day using fresh produce, and a set evening Menu with our pub classics such as Beer Battered Fish and Chips and a homemade Tavern Pie. What better to accompany your meal than a fresh, tasty beer ? One of the first pubs in Bordeaux to collaborate with British brewery Greene King, The Market Tavern has 10 different beers on tap ranging from British Ales to Craft Lager and IPAs. The Market Tavern Bordeaux GastroPub has an international and bilingual staff (The Marketeers) that are always ready and willing to help with any questions customers may have about the beers, the food or the city of Bordeaux.

The Market Tavern menu changes with the season but always offers good, hearty and quality food. With pub classics such as Fish and Chips (using a beer-batter home made with one of our IPAs), a selection of burgers (from classic Cheeseburgers to the Market Special, a double decker burger with pulled pork and onion rings) and some meals that combine the best of French and British cuisine (the British Steak Tartare, made with authentic cheddar cheese or Deep Fried Camembert bites) there is something for everyone. Even if you just want a light bite we have a selection of starters and sharing platters to be enjoyed with friends (such as Pulled Pork Nachos or our Tavern Platter) !
